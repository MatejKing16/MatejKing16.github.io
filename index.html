End-of-Arm Tooling for Bag-in-Box Palletization

Graduation Thesis · March 2026

Robot: FANUC R-1000iA/100F
10 kg
Payload
1.7 kg
EOAT Weight
k = 4.8
Safety Factor
−60 kPa
Vacuum Level

Designed a complete End-of-Arm Tool for palletizing Bag-in-Box packaging using a FANUC industrial robot. Base plate EN-AW 5083 aluminum (8mm). Vacuum system: SMC ZK2A15K5PL-06-B ejector with 4× SMC 50mm anti-slip suction cups (total contact area 6,540 mm²). Digital PNP sensor with hysteresis for grip confirmation. Full technical drawings per ISO 13715, 18-component BOM, economic calculation.

FANUC SolidWorks Vacuum Engineering ISO 13715 EOAT
